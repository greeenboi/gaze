## Unnamed RPG Project

Unnamed RPG is as is. A Roguelite built for exploring the limits of dungeon crawlers and the concept of magic systems.

> Unnamed RPG is a work in progress and is currently under development. Please check back for updates and new features.
> You are still free to use it and contribute to it.

## Prerequisites

- <InlineCode>Ruby >~ 3.3.6</InlineCode>

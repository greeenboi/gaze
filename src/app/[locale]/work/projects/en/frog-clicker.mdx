## Overview

Frog Clicker is a play on the viral lizard clicker game, but with frogs. The game is a simple clicker game where you click on a frog to earn points. You can then use those points to buy upgrades and other items to help you earn more points.



## Try it out

You can play the game directly in your web browser by visiting the following link:
[Play Frog Clicker](https://frog-wizard-guild.vercel.app/)
---

`WASD` to move, `ENTER` to interact, `ESC` to pause/exit.
